#### Common uses of Public Key Certificate
***

- **HTTPS (SSL/TLS):** For secure browsing, protecting data in transit
- **Email Security (S/MIME):** For email encryption and signing
- **Code Signing:** To verify that software or files are from a legitimate source and have not been tampered with
- **VPNs and Remote Access:** For secure access to private networks
