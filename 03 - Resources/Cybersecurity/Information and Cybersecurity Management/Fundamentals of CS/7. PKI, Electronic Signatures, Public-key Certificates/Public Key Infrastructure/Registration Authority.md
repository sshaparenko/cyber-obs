#cybersecurity 

The registration authority (RA) checks if users are eligible for a digital certificate and, if they are, tells the [[Certificate Authority]] to create it. This certificate includes a public key for secure communication and signature verification. If there’s no RA in the network, the [[Certificate Authority]] takes on the RA's role and handles those checks itself.
