**A validation authority** delivers the validation of PKI certificates. 

Certificate validation includes access to Certificate Revocation Lists (CRL), Online Certificate Status Protocol (OCSP) and CA chain certificate downloads

Since certificates can not only be issued but also revoked, it is necessary to confirm their validity
